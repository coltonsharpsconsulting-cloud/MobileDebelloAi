## ⏱️ Match Timing

| Period | Duration | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Autonomous | 15 sec | No driver input. Robot runs pre-programmed routine. |
| Driver Control | 1 min 45 sec | Full driver control. Alliance partners coordinate. |
| End Game | Last 30 sec | Climb bar becomes eligible. |
| **Total Match** | **2 min** | |

## 🤖 Robot Rules — Key Limits

<div class="rule-box">
<h3>Robot Specifications</h3>
<ul>
<li>Max size: 18" × 18" × 18" at start of match</li>
<li>Max 88W total motor power (8 × 11W smart motors)</li>
<li>Must use VEX V5 legal components only</li>
<li>Must pass inspection before competing</li>
<li>Can expand beyond 18" during match (after started)</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="rule-box caution">
<h3>⚠️ Disqualification Risks</h3>
<ul>
<li>Pinning an opponent robot for more than 5 seconds (warning then DQ)</li>
<li>Intentional tipping or flipping opponent robots</li>
<li>Controlling more than one mobile goal at a time (if against current rules)</li>
<li>Entering the opponent's protected zone during Autonomous</li>
<li>Robot starts outside the starting tile</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="rule-box tip">
<h3>✅ Coach Tips</h3>
<ul>
<li>Always have a backup autonomous — a simple one that at least moves</li>
<li>The negative corner is a weapon — trap opponent's mobile goals there</li>
<li>Positive corner control is often worth more than extra rings</li>
<li>Driver Skills and Programming Skills run independently — train both</li>
<li>Engineering Notebook quality matters for judge awards</li>
</ul>
</div>

## 🔍 Robot Inspection Checklist

<ul class="inspect-list">
<li>Robot fits within 18" × 18" × 18" starting size</li>
<li>No more than 88W of motors installed</li>
<li>All motors are VEX V5 Smart Motors</li>
<li>VEX V5 Brain and Controller (no third-party electronics except approved sensors)</li>
<li>No sharp edges or protrusions that could damage the field</li>
<li>Team number displayed clearly on robot</li>
<li>No foam rubber attached to robot (unless legal per Game Manual)</li>
<li>No rubber bands or zip ties used illegally</li>
<li>Battery fully charged (or spare ready)</li>
<li>Autonomous programs loaded and tested</li>
</ul>

## 📣 Match Day Protocol (For Students)

1. **Arrive early** — find your pit, charge batteries, test autonomous on practice field
2. **Queue on time** — missing your queue = match forfeit
3. **Inspect opponent** — note their strengths for alliance strategy
4. **Communicate with alliance partner** — agree on who does what before the match starts
5. **Post-match** — win or lose, shake hands and debrief with your team
